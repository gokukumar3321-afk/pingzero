How to Maximize FPS & Fix Stuttering in Fortnite Chapter 5

Nothing ruins a perfect drop in Fortnite like a sudden frame drop mid-shotgun fight. If your game is stuttering or hitching in Chapter 5, you don't necessarily need a brand-new graphics card...

The Essential Optimizations

  • Switch to Performance Mode: Shift load to your processor and strip away heavy textures.
  • Disable Cosmetic Streaming: Check "Pre-download Streamed Assets" in Epic Games Launcher options.

The Hidden Setting Causing Input Lag in Fortnite Chapter 7

You have high FPS and a great connection—so why does your building still feel delayed? The problem isn't your PC; it's a hidden setting that Windows and Fortnite don't tell you about.

The 2-Minute Delay Fixes

  • Disable HPET in Windows: Right-click High Precision Event Timer in Device Manager and disable it.
  • Enable NVIDIA Reflex: Turn on "On + Boost" inside Fortnite settings.
